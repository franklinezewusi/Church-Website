OUR LEAD PASTOR

Pastor Kayode Oladimeji is the senior pastor of Oasis of Hope Worship Centre, a dynamic ministry based upon the word of God. He has a mandate to raise a Kingdom of Kings and Priests. He believe in bringing people from nowhere to where God wants them to be.
He has serve in various capacity of leadership with a proven testimony of stewardship and integrity. A major speaker in conferences and a prolific writer. His books are sources of godly advice, soul touching with practical principles to give readers and envable adventure in life.
