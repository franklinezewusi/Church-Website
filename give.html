Generous Giving

Partner with us in advancing God's Kingdom through your cheerful giving

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 Corinthians 9:7 (NIV)

At Oasis of Hope Worship Centre, we believe that giving is an act of worship and an expression of our faith. Your generous contributions enable us to spread the Gospel, support our community, and fulfill our mission of raising Kings and Priests.

Biblical Principles of Giving

Cheerfully

God loves a cheerful giver. Give with joy and gratitude in your heart.

Proportionately

Give according to how God has blessed you and as you have purposed in your heart.

Worshipfully

Giving is an act of worship, acknowledging God as the source of all blessings.

Generously

The measure you use for others will be measured back to you.
